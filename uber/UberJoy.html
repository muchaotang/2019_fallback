UBER JOY

Uber app redesigned to leverage riding experience with customized options, utilizing the idle time while waiting for your Uber driver.

Tools: Sketch, Principle

Individual project

 

What do you do while WAITING for your uber?

Nothing important, right?

 

WHAT IF

You can leverage this period to make your ride

MORE ENJOYABLE?

 

What is your top priority in this trip?

  • Speed (fastest route)
  • Comfort (less ups and downs/ less bumps)

Uber respects your priority of the journey, whether you want to reach your destination ASAP, or you want to avoid feeling sick because of the ups and downs in the route. More speedy or more comfortable, it is all your choice.

What do you expect of the ride?

  • Chatting: Quiet/Neutral/Talkative
  • Air conditioning: On/Off
  • Radio: On/Off

Uber takes care of every aspect of your expectations of the journey. You want to rest? We take care of it. You want to chat? Your Uber driver will be more than happy to talk with you. You want the radio or air conditioning to be off? No problem! It is all your choice.
